Brief role description

I am a full-stack software developer working for Abingdon School. My primary responsibility is designing and deploying new web systems for various departments within the school - both for internal use and for parent/pupil use.

For a large portion of my career I have been a solo developer responsible for maintaining existing web system created by my predecessors on top of creating new systems. Seeing how these existing systems excelled and fell short taught me a huge amount about software development and maintainability.

In the summer of 2021, we temporarily hired some talented final-year students for summer development work before they started at university. My responsibilities expanded to include teaching them about our existing systems and our standard practices, along with more general software engineering skills. This experience was enjoyable as teaching new developers is a passion of mine and further prepared me to take on another graduate developer in September 2021.
